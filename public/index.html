⚽ How to play Prompt Cup

A penalty shootout for the dead time while your coding agent works. You can only score while the agent is actually working, and the game stops the instant it finishes.

🏆 Trophy cabinet

1. The pitch is closed until your agent works

No live agent, no goals. Submit a prompt in your coding agent (Claude Code, Codex, or Gemini) and the referee opens the match. Want to warm up with nothing on the line? Hit Play a friendly.

2. Kickoff

The moment you send a prompt, the lamp turns red (AGENT LIVE) and your match begins. The scoreline and the dots up top (the pips) track a best-of-five shootout against an AI nation.

3. Your kick: beat the keeper

Move the mouse (or arrow keys) to aim. Your aim sways, that's nerves, so time it. Click once to start the power bar, click again to shoot:

  • Under 50% tame, the keeper holds it
  • 50 to 72% placed
  • 72 to 90% the sweet spot, hardest to save
  • Over 90% a blast that drifts toward the post

4. Your save: you go in goal

Roles flip every round. The goal splits into three zones and you are the goalkeeper. The shooter commits before you choose, he can't see your dive, so read him: he leans toward his side (the best teams fake it), and from his third kick the ticker shows where he's gone all match. Dive with ← ↑ → or click a zone.

5. The whistle, and the stall warning

When the agent finishes, full time, the pitch freezes and you're back to your review. And if the agent goes silent for 45 seconds (often a permission prompt you didn't notice), the game stops and tells you to check your agent, so it never fakes progress.

The cup

Win to climb GROUP → R16 → QF → SF → FINAL. Lose and you replay the stage. Matches suspend at the whistle and resume on your next prompt, being 3 to 2 up in a semi is what pulls you back next time you code.
